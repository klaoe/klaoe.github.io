The Vegan Brunch

I have been a great fan of the Brunch concept since I lived in NYC and had the first one, it was the year 2000. Back then I used to work for a restaurant called Le Singe Vert, in Chelsea where we used to serve amazing brunches with a gorgeus twist of France, prepared by a really nice and friendly Mexican Chef called Andres and his kitchen staff. Everything was undoubtably it was really good, it wasn't vegan though. I wasn't either. Time has passed since the NYC days I have lived and travel through different countries and in between I have transited from being 100% carnivorous to 100% vegan from one day to the other. It happened in 2012 and it has been a long journey of discovering a whole new universe of food. After 8 years of having changed my eating habits I am able to create a wide selection of vegan brunches, 100% plant based pleasure to delight all palates.

Sometimes you wish for something specific. Brunch is for when you want it all.
