Let see what I do

With more than 25 years of work in the food industry, I have acquired the ability to work in different environments and to understand the ins-and-outs and the workflows of the food and gastronomy industry. As a gastronomic advisor, vegan since 2012, I help people and companies to find business solutions to integrate a compassionate and sustainable way of eating...My job is to help people. I help them in many ways. I am a polyglot that has lived and worked in several countries.
